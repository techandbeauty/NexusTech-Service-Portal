What platforms do you develop for?

We develop native apps for both iOS and Android platforms, as well as cross-platform solutions using React Native and Flutter. Our team ensures optimal performance and user experience on each platform.

+

Development timelines vary based on complexity and features. Simple apps typically take 8-12 weeks, while complex enterprise applications can take 16-24 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during our discovery phase.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive post-launch support including bug fixes, performance optimization, OS updates, and feature enhancements. Our support packages are tailored to your specific needs and budget.

Can you help with App Store submission?

+

Absolutely! We handle the entire App Store and Google Play submission process, including preparing marketing materials, screenshots, descriptions, and ensuring compliance with all platform guidelines.
